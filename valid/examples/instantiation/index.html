Description A small workcell consisting of two simple machines connected by a buffer of capacity 1, also known as "small factory".

Demonstrated		 Templates and instantiation
Projects
TwoMachinesOneBuffer
The small factory model, consisting of two machines and a buffer supervsior.
How to Use Open project TwoMachinesOneBuffer, and open the template machine by clicking on the triangle icon in front of it. The project contains two plants machine.1 and machine.2, representing the two machines of the workcell. Since these two automata have very similar structure, they have been derived from a common template called machine, by substituting the indexes 1 and 2 for the parameter mach_num. Furthermore, a named range called MACHINES has been introduced to represent the range (1..2) of instantiation. A named range can be reused in other instantiations, so that a single change of the range can affect many instantiations simultaneously.
